# Problem

Find a quadratic $$P(x)$$ with real coefficients such that

\\[\left\lvert P(x)+\frac{1}{x-4}\right\rvert\leq0.01,\forall x\in[-1,1].\\]

<!-- SOLUTION_START -->
# Solution
