# Problem

Find the pairs of real numbers $$(p,q)$$ such that $$|\sqrt{1-x^2}-px-q|\leq\frac{1}{2}(\sqrt{2}-1),\forall x\in[0,1]$$.

<!-- SOLUTION_START -->
# Solution

Consider the substitition $$\sqrt{1-x^2}-px=\cos\theta-p\sin\theta$$.
